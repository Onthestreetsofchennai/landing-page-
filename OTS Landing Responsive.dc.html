The method

Couch to Stage

Like Couch-to-5K, but the finish line is a stage. Four checkpoints, day by day — 1:1 coaching at the core, plus a weekly group class with your cohort of 15.

01
Busker · ~day 12

Your hands hurt, then your first riff lands. "I can't" becomes "I played that."

02
Soloist · ~day 30

You can play your whole 3–5 song set, solo, start to finish.

03
Dress · ~day 40

A full run in front of a small room. Nerves, handled.

04
Headliner · day 45

You walk on a real OTS stage and perform. Solo.
